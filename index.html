Auctions & Events

Step into the Auction Chamber, where rare and exquisite works are unveiled under candlelight.

  • Dates & Times: Each auction night begins at twilight, when shadows grow long.
  • Featured Works: Only the most coveted paintings are chosen—pieces whispered about in hushed tones, rumored to carry secrets within their brushstrokes.
  • Registration: Guests must register in advance. Only those who hold a numbered token may enter the bidding circle.

Beyond auctions, Twilight Gallery hosts evenings that blur the line between art and ritual.

  • Candlelit Tours: Visitors wander through the gallery guided only by flickering candles, discovering works that seem to shift in the shadows.
  • Storytelling Evenings: Curators and guest narrators recount the lore behind forbidden paintings—ghost stories, myths, and tales of obsession.
  • The Midnight Gathering: A once-a-year event where collectors, artists, and seekers of the strange convene to share their own haunted creations.

History of Twilight Gallery

"There are places where art is displayed. And then there are places where art is kept… because it cannot be allowed to leave."
Twilight Gallery’s origins are shrouded in rumor, but fragments of its story have survived in journals, letters, and whispered recollections. From its earliest candlelit exhibitions, the gallery became a sanctuary for the extraordinary—paintings that stirred unease, whispered secrets, and seemed alive in their frames.

Over time, it grew into a place of mystery and reverence:

  • The Marketplace of Shadows for works that could be owned.
  • The Auction Chamber for rare pieces unveiled under velvet drapes.
  • The Lore Room, reserved for paintings bound by legend, never to leave the gallery’s walls.

Today, Twilight Gallery endures as both marketplace and myth. It is not merely a place to view art, but to experience it—where every canvas is a story, and every story lingers long after you depart.

"Step inside, and remember: some paintings are meant to be owned… and some are meant to own you."
